How to Become a Member

If you're interested in learning more, here's a few initial steps you can take.

Explore this website!

In particular, see if you align with our mission and vision.

Come to a St. Louis Eco-Village Network event.

The St. Louis Eco-Village Network is a network of people interested in intentional communities, eco-villages, and cooperative living in the St. Louis area (you can think of it as umbrella organization of which Winding Rivers is a part). Connect with them on and .

Come to a potluck or meeting.

Winding Rivers has a monthly social potluck brunch which is a great opportunity to get to know the group members and find out more about our community.
We also meet weekly to work together towards developing group documents and picking and developing a property. First time attendees are welcome to attend and observe the meeting, and once they've identified that they want to be more involved, they are welcome to contribute to the community formation process.

If you're interested in attending either the social brunch or weekly meeting, please get in touch via this contact form and we'll let you know the times and places!
